ℹ️ How It Works

Orpheus 3B is a TTS model built on Llama architecture. It generates SNAC audio tokens instead of text tokens.

LoRA fine-tuning teaches the model to produce audio in YOUR voice style — only ~1% of weights are trained, so it fits on a free Colab T4 GPU.

SNAC codec converts the model's output tokens back into actual audio waveforms at 24kHz quality.
